- Hola - Hello
- Bien - OK
- Esta Bien - How are you?
- Te Amo - I love you
- Te Adoro - I adore you
- Te quiero - I love you
- tu - you
- Y - and (need to make sure this is true - often i hear it as response to esta bien? bien Y tu)
